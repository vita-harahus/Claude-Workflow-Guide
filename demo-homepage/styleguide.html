Typography

One family (Helvetica Neue). Sizes are stepped whole integers per breakpoint — never fluid fractions.
Family

Primary & Secondary — Helvetica Neue (system stack: "Helvetica Neue", Helvetica, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Arial, sans-serif). Headings use tight tracking (−.02 to −.035em); body stays at 0.
