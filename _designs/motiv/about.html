A small studio with a long memory.

We're a senior team of four who would rather do a handful of things properly than a hundred at half-effort. No account layers, no hand-offs — the people you meet are the people who make the work.

Since 2017, building brands that behave like they mean it.

MOTIV started as two designers and a shared frustration: too much work that looked fine and did nothing. So we built a studio around the opposite — identities, sites and motion designed to be felt, then measured.

Today we're a tight team working with founders and in-house leads who want senior craft without agency overhead. We stay small on purpose, so the standard never slips.

Four principles we don't negotiate.
